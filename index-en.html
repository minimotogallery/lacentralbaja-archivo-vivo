03 — Position

We are not trying to fill a calendar. We want a practice to remain long enough to change.

La Central Baja emerged from a specific absence: there are too few intermediate spaces where artistic work can test, fail, produce and gain continuity before becoming an event.

Public programming is a consequence of the process, not a decoration applied to the infrastructure.
